Recipe for Žlikrofi

Description

Idrijski žlikrofi is a traditional dish which comes from, you guessed it, Idrija. It takes some to make them, but it will be worth it in the end. Trust me. Below you will find the ingredients needed to prepare this delicious dish. So, what are you waiting for? Roll up your sleeves and let's get right into it!

Ingredients

  • Dough:
    • flour
    • eggs
    • oil
    • water or milk
  • Filling:
    • potatos
    • bacon
    • onions
  • Spcies:
    • chives
    • black pepper
    • salt
    • marjoram

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients for dough. The finishing product should be "flexible and elastic"
  2. Make the dough into little "breads" and let it rest for 30 min
  3. Cook the potatoes which you will use for filling
  4. Mush the potatoes and add fried bacon and onions
  5. Make little balls from previous step
  6. Roll the dough and gently put the balls on the dough
  7. From the dough make little ears - look the picture above for reference
  8. Voila, you've done it, fucking legend!
  9. Put them in boiling water and wait until they float
  10. Gently scoop them out of the water and serve with some sauce. Perhaps gorgonzola
